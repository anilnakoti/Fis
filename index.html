The Challenge


This is a 48-hours virtual Ideathon open to any startups in APAC having complementary or augmented offerings in line with FIS Ideathon themes. The participant will study FIS ecosystem using publicly available products and API documentation. Based on the Ideathon themes, participants will define suitable problem statements. Submission will include a theme-specific pitch deck, describing specific problem statements chosen and how partner IP offering either standalone or via FIS API’s / Market place can bring innovative solutions. Partners can also suggest new FIS API’s that may help to enhance the solution further.

WHO SHOULD PARTICIPATE ?

  1. Startups with a technology stack that can solve horizontal problems. Customer onboarding, churn detection, customer loyalty, data marketplaces, DeFi stacks.
    Example : Health care startup that has developed innovative ways to share private data securely and build new AIML models and want to work with FIS to apply similar approach to solve FinTech problems.
  2. FinTech Startups with innovative business models that can augment FIS offering and want broader reach.
  3. Startups aspiring to build products and platforms leveraging FIS API’s.
  4. Group of startups collaborating and providing new capabilities.
    Example : Satellite imaging, Drone startups and radar startup collaborate and provide alternate data for commodities markets.
  5. Startups having innovating automation and integration platforms.
  6. Program is mainly focused on early-stage startups.
  7. Outreach list includes–FIS Accelerator program startups, FIS Alliance Partner Program startups, FIS Product Management Partners, Vendor Startup Database
